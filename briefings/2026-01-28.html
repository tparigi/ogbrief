Chevron Seeks Better Terms for Iraq's West Qurna 2

  • Chevron is pushing Iraq to improve returns on the West Qurna 2 oilfield before buying the project from Russia's Lukoil (Reuters)
  • Iraq nationalized the field earlier this month after U.S. sanctions on Lukoil
  • West Qurna 2 accounts for ~0.5% of global oil supply and ~10% of Iraq's output
  • Lukoil has until February 28 to sell its assets under U.S. sanctions
  • Any new deal would require Iraqi cabinet approval; negotiations ongoing
  • Would mark further Chevron expansion into Iraq after its $53B Hess acquisition in 2025

Canada-India Energy Dialogue Restarts

  • Canadian Energy Minister Tim Hodgson at India Energy Week announced plans to boost oil, gas, and uranium exports to India (Reuters)
  • Called Canada's 98% energy export reliance on U.S. a "strategic blunder"
  • First Canada-India energy dialogue in 8 years
  • Canada currently does not export crude or LNG to India
  • Canada building pipelines to West Coast; sees India's nuclear push as opportunity for uranium exports
